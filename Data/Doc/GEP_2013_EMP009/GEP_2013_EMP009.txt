EP1300A2
State Government Employment and Payroll Data: March 2013

Release Date : 12/19/2014

Last Revised: 4/26/16

Data users who create their own estimates using these data should cite the U.S. Census Bureau as the source of the original data only. The data in this table are based on information from public records and contain no confidential data. The data in this table come from a sample of governmental units and are thus subject to both sampling and nonsampling error. Additional information on nonsampling error, response rates, and definitions may be found within the survey methodology.

 
  
  Table Name 
  State Government Employment and Payroll Data: March 2013 
  
  
  Universe 
  The universe includes all agencies of the 50 state governments.  
  
  
  GeographyCoverage 
  Data are shown at the U.S. level and the state level. 
  
  
  IndustryCoverage 
  Data are shown at the functional categories for employment statistics, as defined by the 2006 Government Finance and Employment Classification Manual.  
  
  
  Data ItemsandOtherIdentifyingRecords 
  This file contains data on: 
    
    Full-Time Employment 
    Full-Time Payroll (whole dollars) 
    Part-Time Employment 
    Part-Time Payroll (whole dollars) 
    Part-Time Hours 
    Full-Time Equivalent Employment 
    Total March Full-Time and Part-Time Employment 
    Total March Payroll (whole dollars) 
     
  
  
  Sort Order 
  Data are sorted by employment function
